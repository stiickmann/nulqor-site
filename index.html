Product info

Forge Studio is planned as a guided desktop creation app.

The goal is a workspace that feels powerful enough for serious 3D creation, but cleaner and easier to understand when creators get stuck.

  • Electron + Three.js desktop application direction
  • Modeling, rigging, posing, animation, materials, and export
  • AI help for workflow guidance, cleanup, and troubleshooting
  • Future cloud saves, templates, marketplace, and collaboration

Core intelligence

Complex engines. Simple control.

The user sees creation. Nulqor handles the hard parts: rigging checks, topology cleanup, animation fixes, material generation, and export guidance through intelligent systems behind the interface.

Rig doctor Detects weak joints and broken pose behavior.
Topology cleaner Finds messy geometry before it becomes production debt.
Material generator Turns direction into reusable surface systems.
Animation cleanup Refines rough poses into cleaner motion.
