Product info

Forge Studio
guides the build.

Forge Studio brings modeling, rigging, animation, materials, and export into one guided desktop workspace with AI help built around the creator instead of replacing them. The goal is a workspace powerful enough for serious 3D creation, but cleaner and easier to understand when creators get stuck.

MO

Model

Shape objects, parts, scenes, and reusable 3D assets.

RI

Rig

Build skeletons, test poses, and diagnose rig issues.

AN

Animate

Use a timeline built for poses, motion, cleanup, and export.

MA

Materials

Create surfaces, shaders, effects, and visual styles.

SA

Save

Save, load, organize, and eventually sync projects to cloud.

EX

Export

Move work into basic and advanced formats as projects mature.

AI

Ask AI

Get guidance when something breaks or a workflow gets unclear. It will not model for you.

ET

Extend

Prepare for templates, marketplace assets, and team libraries.
